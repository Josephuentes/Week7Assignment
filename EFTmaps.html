The Tarkov Map patch 12.12

In this current patch, BattleStateGames has added the map Lighthouse.

Maps available in the current patch

  • Customs
  • Factory
  • Woods
  • Interchange
  • Reserve
  • Shoreline
  • The Lab

When you start the game you cannot bring up a map in game. You are only given locations where you can extract from the raid with the loot and gear you find.

There maps that others have made that shows the map layout and where the extracts are.

This is the Customs map. The traders in Tarkov give you tasks to complete in the Customs Map as you progress through the tasks.

The trader Prapor wants you to find his friend's bronze pocket watch in Customs. This is one task that requires you to go onto Customs.

As of now there are 8 traders in the game that give you tasks and trade different supplies.
