Welcome to Tarkov

Escape from Tarkov is a first person shooter game with a focus on survival with realism.

BattleStateGames is currently still developing Escape from Tarkov. This game is still in beta.

This game takes place in the Norvinsk region of russia in a fictional city called Tarkov.

You play as one of two pmc factions, USEC or BEAR. USEC are US pmcs and BEAR are Russian pmcs.

When you play the game you go into a raid instance where other players spawn around the map with you.

When in raid you loot areas of the map with other players doing the same thing.

There are AI roaming the map known as Scavs (Scavengers) that will shoot on sight.

With the loot you attain in raid you either sell it to traders or build your hideout to craft food, medical supplies or bullets.
